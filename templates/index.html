Features

Oreo Collection

Collect different types of Oreos from common to legendary rarities. Each Oreo has unique properties and value!

Growing Tree

Nurture your Oreo Tree to produce more and rarer Oreos. Water it daily and watch it grow!

Trading System

Trade Oreos with other server members to complete your collection or get the rarest varieties.

Server Leaderboard

Compete with other members to have the most valuable Oreo collection and climb the leaderboard!

Daily Rewards

Claim daily rewards to help your Oreo Tree grow faster and produce better Oreos.

Special Events

Participate in special limited-time events to earn exclusive Oreo varieties!

Commands

Here are some of the commands you can use with The Oreo Tree bot:

Command Description
$plant Start your Oreo Tree journey and plant your first tree
$water Water your Oreo Tree (can be done once every 12 hours)
$harvest Harvest Oreos from your tree
$collection View your current Oreo collection
$trade @user [oreo] [quantity] Offer to trade Oreos with another user
$daily Claim your daily reward
$upgrade [feature] Upgrade your tree to increase production or quality
$leaderboard View the server's Oreo collection leaderboard
$shop Visit the shop to buy tree upgrades and special items
$help Display a list of all available commands
